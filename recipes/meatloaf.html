Meatloaf

Description

The best thing since sliced bread and oddly similar.

Meatloaf!

Who doesn't like a giant loaf made of meat and all your favorite spices with a spectacular sauce coating the top.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of dried bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of dry mustard
  • 1/3 cup of ketchup
