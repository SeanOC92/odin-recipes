Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Do you like bacon? Who doesn't! How about Jalapenos? Of course you do! Well, do we have a treat for you!

Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeno Poppers!

The ultimate appetizer for any occasion! These jalapenos are cut in half, stuffed with a delicious cheddar cream cheese mixture, wrapped in bacon and baked to perfection

Ingredients

  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise with the seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup of shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended and then stuff each jalapeno half with the mixture.
  4. Put the jalapeno halves back together and wrapped the now stuffed peppers with a slice of bacon.
  5. Place the bacon-wrapped jalapenos on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven unti the bacon is crispy, approximately 15 minutes.
