Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

This recipe is the family favorite that your Grandma used to make everytime you would visit! They are the biggest, fluffiest and best tasting pancakes around!

Simply follow the step by step instructions below using 7 easy ingredients and be transported back to Grandma's house!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
