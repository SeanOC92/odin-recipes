Meatloaf

Description

The best thing since sliced bread and oddly similar.

Meatloaf!

Who doesn't like a giant loaf made of meat and all your favorite spices with a spectacular sauce coating the top.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of dried bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of dry mustard
  • 1/3 cup of ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk, bread crumbs and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Place the mixture in a lightly greased 9x5-inch load pan, or form into a loaf and place it in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. In a seperate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
