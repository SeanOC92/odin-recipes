Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Description

This recipe is the family favorite that your Grandma used to make everytime you would visit! They are the biggest, fluffiest and best tasting pancakes around!

Simply follow the step by step instructions below using 7 easy ingredients and be transported back to Grandma's house!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, melted butter and mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or fryingh pan over meduim-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle or frying pan, using approximately 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both side and serve hot.
