Bananas Foster

Description

Bananas, ice cream and a rum butter caramel sauce! What's not to love!

Don't forget the crunchy and delicious walnuts!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2/3 cup of dark brown sugar
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons of rum
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 peeled bananas sliced length and crosswise
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 pint of vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. In a large deep skillet over meduim heat, melt the butter.
  2. Stir in the sugar, rum, vanilla and cinnamon.
  3. When the mixture begins to bubble, place bananas and walnuts in the pan.
  4. Cook until the bananas are hot, about 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Serve at once over vanilla icre cream.
